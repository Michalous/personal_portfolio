My Work

Below, you will find a selection of projects that showcase my skills and interests in software development. Whether it's my first attempt with hangman made in Flask or a little website for a fictitious product, I am constantly striving to learn and create meaningful work that makes an impact.

Equinox countdown

Nice little project to show a countdown until next equinox. The biggest challenge here for me was to figure out how to display digits in the grid.

Covid cases graph

This project pulls the data from the UK Government website and displays it as a bar chart using Plotly JS. To smooth out the spikes in the data I've calculated 7 day averages and displayed them alongside the actual data.

Mastermind

I got an idea for this game while watching an introductory lesson to CS50AI where it is mentioned. It's something I used to play as a little kid. Maybe the next step for me would be to write an algorithm for computer to win any game - it should be possible in 5 or fewer turns.

Porfolio website version 1.0

My first attempt to build portfolio website. Since it's been built not that long ago it's very similar to this one. Here you will find links to my other little projects.

Playing with Math.random()

It's very satisfying to watch computer compute stuff. Here are four little projects built with Math.random(). They are Monte Carlo integration, probabilities of rolling 2 dice, the birthday problem and shuffling the deck of cards.
